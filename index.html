Mission

Our staff, with their top-notch services, have a mission to familiarize you with the Filipino culture in Cebu's unique ways of caring, provide you infos, with the point of view of the locals, that enhance your stay in the local settings. We strive to provide you with reliable, durable, and quality care in TLC that contribute to memorable adventures and lasting memories, a way different from the generic Airbnb experience. Why? because you will live among US in a house and still preserve your privacy.

Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to destination for Cebu enthusiasts, known for our extensive thorough and premium advices for places to visit with our exceptional local guides who would not hesistate to let you try our terrifying motorbike rides, local two-wheeled taxis, known as Habal-habal and would also let you try our local cuisines such as adobo, pancit and balut. We aspire to inspire and enable people to embrace the beauty of our Cebuano culture and create unforgettable stay experiences with us. We will turn - you- into a smiling person as Cebuanos do. The smiles we give you will be hard to wipe off on your faces,too, long after you leave our island.
