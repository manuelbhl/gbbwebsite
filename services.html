      To get to our property from airport, we have a pick-up service from Mactan International Airport (CEB) for a fee to cover for the GAS and DRIVER'S effort. For $10 fee for the 25 kilometer 45 minutes stretch of a drive from the airport, plus heavy trafiic this will be your best option to save time and money. This will ensure you that you will reach to your destination to our property in Compostela. The Normal taxi will cost at least $20 or P1000 pesos. Please inform us in your reservation that you will need this service. Contact us directly to inquire about this service, as we offer complimentary service with your room "sometimes". You can also use Cebu's online travel transport such as GRAB or Maxim or regular taxi to transport you to our place.

      We have wifi but it is not totally free. There is a free Internet for the first day only. We employ a piso lang internet access, a local service that provides internet as per use. It is a machine located at the reception where you put in an amount of fee for the duration of your use. The internet cost is 60 pesos per day per device for the 512 kbps, and 150 pesos per day per device for the 4 MB internet service.

      There are so many interesting places to visit in Cebu. Our tour guides primary are from the companies that we recommend . Their duties involve leading visitors on tours, providing engaging information about local attractions, ensuring group safety, and managing logistics like transportation and itineraries. They are also well-versed in Cebu's landscape and local cultural norms.They also answer questions, assist and suggest potential unforgettable experience-drenched but safe places to go. They will try safeguarding you from scammers, thieves and hustlers.

      There are two bus terminals in Cebu, (1) North Bus Terminal and (2) South Bus Terminal. These terminals are the starting points that will take you outside Cebu City and also, potentially, take you out of the Cebu island where you will take a barge across waters to the neighboring popular islands such as Camotes and Siquijor.Or to Dumaguete. These buses are air conditioned and give you comfortable rides. To name the most popular bus series are Ceres Bus and Corominas systems . The bus routes are very popular and well known in Cebu's touring places, either to visit to the north or south. WE are sure you will find the most exciting places for snorkling and beaches to hide away in these popular routes.
